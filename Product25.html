Coconut solid lotion bar in a reusable tin. This solid lotion bar is a luxurious blend of natural ingredients. Its tropical, fruity scent of creamy coconut and buttery rich vanilla is perfect for indulging your senses and transporting you to a warm, sunny beach.

The convenient reusable tin is perfect for use at home or on the go. It's travel-friendly and easy to use. Simply take the bar out of the tin and rub it directly onto the skin in the areas desired. This listing is for one 2.5 ounces solid lotion bar that is handmade with natural ingredients. It's coconut scented and perfect for use as a hand and body lotion.
