Relax Shower Steamers

$16.00

    Instructions:

  1. Begin shower as usual.
  2. Open an individually wrapped shower steamer and place on the floor of your shower stall away from the direct stream of water. Do not submerge in puddle of water.
  3. Allow the steam and warm water splashes from your shower water to activate the steamer.
  4. As the steamer slowly dissolves, enjoy the aromas of the menthol and essential oils.

    5. Keep away from children and pets.

    Keep unused shower steamers in resealable bag and away from moisture to preserve scent strength.

Relax Shower Steamers with Lavender, Eucalyptus, & Menthol are amazing tablets that you can add to your ordinary shower routine to achieve a very invigorating and refreshing shower spa experience. Heat from the shower and the warm water activate the shower bomb to create fizz and release the aromatic essential oils to create a sense of tranquility. Transform the everyday shower to a more soothing way to start or end the day by using Relax shower melts.

This listing is for a resealable bag containing four (4) shower steamers that weigh over 2 oz each. That's a total of over 8 oz in a resealable bag.

Each shower steamer is individually shrink-wrapped to preserve the scent for each steamer. This should maintain the scent strength.

Ingredients: Baking soda, citric acid, white kaolin clay, corn startch, menthol crystals, eucalyptus essential oil, lavender essential oil, skin safe mica minerals for coloring, Polysorbate 80

Scent: Lavender Essential Oil, Eucalyptus Essential Oil, and Menthol Crystals.

SHOWER USE ONLY. DO NOT USE FOR SOAKING IN THE BATH.
THIS IS NOT A BATH BOMB!
The strong menthol and essential oils could be harmful if used directly on the skin.
